# Rolling in the Dough — Design Brainstorm

## Concept
A sweepstakes-style slot machine game with a baking/bread/dough theme. The name "Rolling in the Dough" plays on both money and literal bread dough, giving us a rich visual language to work with.

## Idea 1: Gilded Bakery — Art Deco Opulence

**Design Movement**: Art Deco meets Las Vegas casino glamour with a baking twist

**Core Principles**:
- Lavish gold and deep burgundy palette evoking wealth and indulgence
- Geometric ornamental borders and symmetrical layouts
- Every element feels "expensive" — heavy shadows, metallic sheens, embossed textures
- Bread/dough motifs rendered in gold filigree style

**Color Philosophy**:
- Background: Deep midnight navy (#0a0a1a)
- Primary accent: Rich gold (#D4AF37) and warm amber (#C8860A)
- Secondary: Burgundy (#6B1A2A) and cream (#F5E6C8)
- Emotional intent: Wealth, excitement, luxury, indulgence

**Layout Paradigm**:
- Centered machine with dramatic vertical symmetry
- Flanked by ornate column-like side panels with animated coin showers
- Header marquee with scrolling gold text
- Asymmetric info panels on left/right sides

**Signature Elements**:
- Art Deco geometric border patterns around the reel window
- Gold coin particle explosions on wins
- Ornate "Rolling in the Dough" logotype with serif letterforms and gold gradient

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Every button press triggers a satisfying tactile "click" with micro-animation
- Lever pull animation for the spin button
- Coins cascade and bounce on wins

**Animation**:
- Reels spin with motion blur, decelerate with elastic easing
- Win lines illuminate with golden glow pulses
- Jackpot: full-screen coin shower with confetti
- Idle: subtle shimmer on gold elements

**Typography System**:
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, ornate) for logo and headings
- Body: Cormorant Garamond for secondary text
- Numbers: Oswald for coin counts and win amounts

## Selected Design: **Idea 1 — Gilded Bakery (Art Deco Opulence)**

This approach best captures the "Rolling in the Dough" dual meaning — literal bread dough AND rolling in money. The Art Deco casino aesthetic is immediately recognizable as a slot machine game while the gold/amber palette and baking-themed symbols create a unique, memorable identity.
